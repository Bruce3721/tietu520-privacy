小明贴纸 / Sticker Craft

Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

Last updated: June 2026

Overview / 概述

Sticker Craft respects your privacy. This app processes photos on your device to create stickers.

小明贴纸重视您的隐私。App 在您的设备上处理照片以制作贴纸。

Information We Use / 使用的信息

  • Photos and camera images you choose
  • Sticker data stored on your device
  • Optional iCloud sync (VIP)
  • Sign in with Apple identifier (optional)
  • Subscription status from Apple (VIP)

How We Use It / 用途

To create stickers, save and sync them, unlock VIP features, and support iMessage and widgets. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

用于制作、保存和同步贴纸，解锁 VIP 功能，并提供 iMessage 与小组件支持。我们不会出售您的数据，也不用于广告。

Storage / 存储

Processing happens on device. VIP iCloud sync uses your private iCloud container.

处理在设备本地完成。VIP iCloud 同步使用您的私有 iCloud 容器。

Your Choices / 您的选择

Manage permissions in iOS Settings, delete stickers in the app, disable iCloud sync, sign out, or cancel subscriptions in Settings → Subscriptions.

可在 iOS 设置中管理权限，在 App 内删除贴纸，关闭 iCloud 同步，退出登录，或在「设置 → 订阅」中取消订阅。

Contact / 联系

Contact us through the App Store listing for Sticker Craft.

请通过 App Store 中小明贴纸应用页面联系我们。
